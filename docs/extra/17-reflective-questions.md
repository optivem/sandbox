# Reflective Questions

Please write these questions and your answers to them, regarding your Real Life Project:

1. Is it Single Component (Monolith) or Multiple Components (Frontend & Backend)?
2. Is it Mono Repo or Multi Repo?
3. Is it public-facing UI, or public-facing REST API, or both? or something else too? (e.g. CLI, Mobile)
4. Regarding integration with external systems, aside from integrating with external REST API, is there anything else you integrate with externally (e.g. SOAP, gRPC, FTP)
5. Does your tech stack (incl. DB choice) reflect what you would do in your real life work?
6. If you were to present this sandbox to your colleagues, would they see it as an adequate mirror of key technical characteristics (tech stack, architecture) of your real life project, so that when you use this project as a demo, that the learnings could be translated to real life project? Or is there something where they might say "that won't translate to our real life project"?
